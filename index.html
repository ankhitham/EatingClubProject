THE DAILY PRINCETONIAN | title

The Daily Princetonian analyzed eating club tax returns over the past 16 years to discover how different eating clubs managed their budgets. The dataset was drawn from each eating club’s publicly available Form 990, which all income tax-exempt organizations are required to file. The Form 990s detail everything about an eating club’s finances, from stock investments to expenditures for food and beverages. In this project, we visualized our findings from this data.
